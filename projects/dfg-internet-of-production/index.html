Internet of Production Excellence Cluster

The vision of the Internet of Production strives for the integration of the major domains of a producing company along the lifecycle of a product (production, development, and usage). This integration, enabled by the Infrastructure of the Internet of Production, will result in a new level and understanding of cross-domain collaboration due to real-time availability of semantically adequate and contextual data from these domains.

Center for Systems Engineering

The Center for Systems Engineering aims to shape and holistically assess the required key technologies for tomorrow’s product development process. The integration of the individual development steps and tools enables a highly iterative, agile, and in part automated product development. This will facilitate model-based systems engineering in research and practice.
